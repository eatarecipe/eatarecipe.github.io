# Cornbread

## ingredients
* 1/2 c. cornmeal
* 1 1/2 c. flour
* 2/3 c. sugar
* 1 TBS baking powder
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/3 c. oil
* 3 TBS melted butter
* 1 TBS honey
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 1 1/4 c. milk

## steps  
1. Mix dry ingredients together
2. Add wet ingredients, mix.
3. Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes
4. Top with butter or honey butter
