# Christmas Chip Dip

## ingredients
* 8 oz cream cheese
* 12 oz cottage cheese
* 12 oz sour cream
* 12 oz miracle whip (not mayo)
* catsup
* canned tiny shrimp, crab, or both
* tobasco sauce

* Ruffles wavy chips

## steps  
1. equal parts cottage cheese, sour cream, and miracle whip
2. catsup for color
3. tobasco for heat
