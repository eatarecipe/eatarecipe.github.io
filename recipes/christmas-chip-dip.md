## ingredients
* cream cheese
* cottage cheese
* sour cream
* miracle whip (not mayo)
* catsup
* canned tiny shrimp, crab, or both
* tobasco sauce

* Ruffles wavy chips


## steps  
1. equal parts cottage cheese, sour cream, and miracle whip
2. catsup for color
3. tobasco for heat
