# Marcia's Clam Chowder

## info  
* 
## ingredients
* 2 cans minced Clams  
* 1 cup onions
* 1 cup diced celery
* 2 cups diced potatoes  
* 3/4 c. butter 
* 3/4 c. white flour  
* 1 quart half & half  
* 2 cups milk  
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1/8 tsp pepper 

## steps  
1. Cook vegetables in clam juice with water to cover white sauce with.
2. Cook until thick.
